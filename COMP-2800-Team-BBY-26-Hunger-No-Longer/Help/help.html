Welcome to FAQ

This is where your most commonly asked questions are answered.

Q: How do I login?

A: Users can login using their email and password. First time users will have to enter a first and last name along with choosing a password.


Q: How do I logout?

A: Users can logout using the logout button that is found at the bottom of the sidebar. The sidebar can be opened using the hamburger menu at the top left.


Q: How do I sign up as a volunteer?

A: After signing up you have to create an account as an individual. Next you can access the sidebar and from there you may click on the volunteering page. At the volunteering page you will have to fill out a form to become a volunteer for a charity.


Q: How do I find out more about the devs of the app?

A: Information on the app's developers can be found in the "About Us" page. This page can be accessed through the hamburger menu at the top left of the home page.


Q: How can I change my username?

A: A user's username can be changed through the "My Account" page which is found in the hamburger menu. On this page users can click on edit profile then enter a new username in the box provided.


Q: How can I access the map?

A: Assuming you are logged in and on the home page you may click on the blue pin beside "Search in map" at the top left of the screen. This will redirect the user to the map page. From here you can navigate the map in your area and click on any of the pins to see the name.


Q: How can I create a post?

A: Assuming you are logged in and on the home page you open the navigation bar by clicking the hamburger menu on the top left. Next you click on social hub and are redirected to the Social Hub. From here a "post" button will appear at the bottom of the page. After clicking on this button the user will be able to create a post by filling in the title and body of the post they wish to create.


Q: How can I post my leftover food?

A: Assuming you are logged in and on the home page you open the navigation bar by clicking the hamburger menu on the top left. Next you click on post news and after being redirected you may choose your food name, post a picture of the food or enter the best before date. If you make a mistake at this page you may also reset your entries by clicking the reset button. When everything is to your liking you can finally click the submit button.


Q: What does this app do?

A: Hunger No Longer is an application that is made to reduce starvation and as the name suggests, hunger. We hope to bring a change in our community through reducing food waste by connecting businesses and charities together. We also provide an oppurtunity for individuals to volunteer by helping out with the transport of food between business and charity. To find out more visit our About Us page!
