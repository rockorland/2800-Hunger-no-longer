ABOUT US
Our Goal
At Hunger No Longer, we are a group of computer science students at BCIT in the CST program who has come together to develop an application that would connect businesses, charities, and volunteers in order to curb food waste and hunger

Over half a million people go hungry in BC. Food waste is one of the major contributors in global warming and local hunger here in Vancouver. How about the thought of being able to donate the food or sell it at a discount? Our solution is to provide a simple subscription for businesses to sell food at a discount or even donate at no cost. We hope this will bring in more businesses and connect them with local charities in curbing food waste and ending hunger.
